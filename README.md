This project is a simple OOP exercise that simulates shopping at a market.
The program takes two input lines followed by purchase commands:

People with their budget (e.g. Pesho=11;Gosho=4)
Products with their price (e.g. Bread=10;Milk=2)
Purchase commands until END (e.g. Pesho Bread)

It validates that:

Names cannot be empty
Money cannot be negative

For each purchase it outputs:

Whether the person bought the product or couldn't afford it

After all purchases it outputs:

A summary of what each person bought, or "Nothing bought" if they made no purchases
